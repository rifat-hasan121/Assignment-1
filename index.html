Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation drives progress and opens doors to new possibilities, transforming industries and economies.

Sustainability

Sustainability is crucial for ensuring a balanced future, where economic growth, environmental protection, and social well-being coexist harmoniously.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure is the backbone of development, providing the essential foundation for transportation, communication, and economic growth.

Empowerment

Empowerment enables individuals and communities to gain control over their lives, fostering confidence and the ability to create positive change.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Embracing the Digital Revolution

Developing an inclusive digital platform to empower communities with resources and opportunities.

Digital Empowerment

Building a robust national digital infrastructure to ensure seamless access to education, healthcare, and government services for every citizen.
